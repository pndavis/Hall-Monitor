Bouquet Gardens

Located a few blocks from the heart of campus, Bouquet Gardens accommodates 651 students in 172 units in nine buildings.

Each air-conditioned unit is furnished and typically includes four single bedrooms. Bouquet J offers four bedroom apartments, but also includes some three person and two person apartments as well. The two and three person apartments are made up of single bedrooms and one bathroom. All four person apartments in Bouquet Gardens include two bathrooms.

Bouquet Gardens Apartments include a kitchen with refrigerator, stove and microwave, a living room and dining area. In addition to these amenities, Bouquet J includes a flat screen television in each living room of every apartment.

Parking is not available at Bouquet Gardens.

Bedroom dimensions: 9'9" x 10'3"

Bathroom dimensions: 7'5" x 7'5" (two in each four person apartment and one in two/three person apartments)

Bouquet J is home to the Bouquet Gardens student mailroom, fitness center and bike storage. All residents of Bouquet Gardens have access to each of these amenities.
